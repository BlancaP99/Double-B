WHO WE ARE
A studio built on
strategy + imagination.

We are a multidisciplinary design studio founded by Blanca and Beatriz Palazuelo, focused on crafting bold, contemporary visual identities. Our work spans motion graphics, graphic design, web design, branding, marketing, CRM, and social media direction, always with a strong creative perspective. We deliberately move away from minimal and generic solutions, embracing modern twists, expressive colour palettes, and surreal, other-worldly aesthetics that set brands apart in a saturated market.

Blanca Palazuelo contributes a distinctive creative vision rooted in graphic design, motion graphics, web design, branding, and creative direction; while Beatriz brings solid experience from corporate environments, with a background in marketing strategy, CRM, social media management, and graphic design. Together, we combine strategy and imagination to create design systems that feel alive, intentional, and unique.
